About Us

Centre de formation au brevet technicien supérieur en :
comptabilité et gestion, Développement des systèmes d'information, Intelligence Artificielle.

comptabilité et gestion

is an academic and professional specialization that combines the fields of accounting and management. Students specializing in this field acquire skills in both accounting, financial analysis, and business management.

Développement des systèmes d'information

is a field that focuses on the design, creation, implementation, and maintenance of computer-based information systems within organizations. This field combines elements of computer science, information technology, and management to develop systems that support the operational and strategic goals of an organization.

Intelligence Artificielle

is a branch of computer science that focuses on creating systems or machines capable of performing tasks that would typically require human intelligence. These tasks include learning, reasoning, problem-solving, perception, language understanding, and decision-making.
